sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: It was the fastest in an sorted list. It was the worst in reversed and random lists.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: It was the fastest in all random and reversed lists. The speed was always linear at all sizes.

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?:  It is the slowest in an sorted list. In random lists it was slower then Merge Sort but better than Bubble Sort.
